Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-23  ·  Last updated: 2026-05-23

This Privacy Policy describes how Tung Lai Quang ("we", "us", "our"), as an independent individual developer, collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Finio mobile application ("Finio", "the app").

If you do not agree with this Policy, please do not use Finio.

1. Who we are

Finio is operated by Tung Lai Quang, an independent individual developer based in Vietnam. We are the data controller for the personal data described below.

For any privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at:

  • Email: laji1110@gmail.com

2. Information we collect

Finio collects only the information needed to provide a personal finance tracking service. We do not buy personal data from third parties, and we do not collect data through hidden tracking.

2.1 Account information

When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive:

  • Your unique account identifier from the identity provider
  • Your email address
  • Your name (only if you choose to share it with Apple Sign-In; Google always provides it)
  • Your profile picture URL, if available

This information is used to create and identify your Finio account.

2.2 Profile information you provide

You may optionally provide:

  • Full name (editable in Settings → Personal Info)
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth

2.3 Financial data you enter

Finio is a personal finance app. All financial data is manually entered by you. We do not import or sync data from banks, brokers, or wallets. This includes:

  • Assets you record (cash, stocks, gold, real estate, crypto, other)
  • Asset transactions (buy / sell records you log)
  • Income sources and amounts
  • Expense categories and amounts
  • Monthly budget figures
  • Financial goals (target amount, target date, monthly contribution)
  • Onboarding preferences (preferred asset classes, investment style, primary financial goals)

2.4 Technical information

  • Authentication tokens issued by Supabase and the identity providers
  • A "last active" timestamp stored locally on your device for inactivity auto-logout
  • Standard server logs from our backend provider (Supabase), which may include IP address and request timestamps for security purposes

2.5 What we do not collect

  • We do not collect device contacts, photos, location, microphone, or camera data.
  • We do not collect data from your bank accounts, brokerage accounts, or wallets.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs that profile you across apps.
  • We do not use advertising identifiers (IDFA).

3. How we use your information

We use your information only to:

  • Authenticate you and keep your session secure
  • Display and compute your portfolio, budgets, and financial goals inside the app
  • Render charts and basic insights based on data you have entered
  • Provide customer support when you contact us
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data to train AI models, to build user profiles for advertising, or to make automated decisions that have legal effects on you.

4. Legal basis for processing

For users in jurisdictions where this concept applies (e.g., EU/EEA users under GDPR), our legal bases are:

  • Performance of a contract — to provide the Finio service you requested.
  • Consent — for optional features. You can withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account.
  • Legitimate interests — to keep the service secure and prevent abuse.

6. Data retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your Finio account is active.

When you delete your account (Settings → Delete Account), we trigger a cascade deletion of:

  • Your user profile
  • Your assets, asset price history, and asset transactions
  • Your income sources and expense categories
  • Your transactions and monthly budgets
  • Your financial goals and onboarding profile
  • Your Supabase Auth identity

After deletion, residual copies may remain in encrypted database backups for up to 30 days, after which they are permanently removed by our backend provider's standard backup rotation.

Server access logs are retained by our backend provider according to their standard retention policy.

7. Your rights

Regardless of your jurisdiction, you can exercise the following rights inside the app or by contacting us:

  • Access — view your data inside the app at any time.
  • Correction — edit your profile and financial entries.
  • Deletion — permanently delete your account and all associated data via Settings → Delete Account.
  • Withdraw consent — by deleting your account.

If you are an EU/EEA, UK, California, or other resident with statutory data protection rights, you also have:

  • The right to data portability (request a copy)
  • The right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority
  • The right to object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, email us at laji1110@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Transport encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all network traffic
  • Database-level Row Level Security (RLS) so that one user cannot read another user's records
  • OAuth-based authentication (no passwords stored in the app)
  • Session inactivity timeout that signs you out after 30 days of inactivity

No system is 100% secure. If we become aware of a personal data breach affecting you, we will notify you and the appropriate authorities as required by applicable law.

9. Children's privacy

Finio is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. International data transfers

Because our backend provider Supabase operates global infrastructure on AWS, your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States and other AWS regions. By using Finio, you consent to this transfer.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated through the app or by email when feasible. Continued use of Finio after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
