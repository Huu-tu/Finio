## 2. Information we collect

Finio collects only the information needed to provide a personal finance
tracking service. We do not buy personal data from third parties, and we do not
collect data through hidden tracking.

### 2.1 Account information

When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive:

- Your unique account identifier from the identity provider
- Your email address
- Your name (only if you choose to share it with Apple Sign-In; Google
  always provides it)
- Your profile picture URL, if available

This information is used to create and identify your Finio account.

### 2.2 Profile information you provide

You may optionally provide:

- Full name (editable in Settings → Personal Info)
- Phone number
- Date of birth

### 2.3 Financial data you enter

Finio is a personal finance app. All financial data is **manually entered by
you**. We do not import or sync data from banks, brokers, or wallets. This
includes:

- Assets you record (cash, stocks, gold, real estate, crypto, other)
- Asset transactions (buy / sell records you log)
- Income sources and amounts
- Expense categories and amounts
- Monthly budget figures
- Financial goals (target amount, target date, monthly contribution)
- Onboarding preferences (preferred asset classes, investment style,
  primary financial goals)

### 2.4 Technical information

- Authentication tokens issued by Supabase and the identity providers
- A "last active" timestamp stored locally on your device for inactivity
  auto-logout
- Standard server logs from our backend provider (Supabase), which may
  include IP address and request timestamps for security purposes

### 2.5 What we do **not** collect

- We do not collect device contacts, photos, location, microphone, or
  camera data.
- We do not collect data from your bank accounts, brokerage accounts, or
  wallets.
- We do not use analytics SDKs that profile you across apps.
- We do not use advertising identifiers (IDFA).

## 6. Data retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your Finio account is active.

When you delete your account (Settings → Delete Account), we trigger a
cascade deletion of:

- Your user profile
- Your assets, asset price history, and asset transactions
- Your income sources and expense categories
- Your transactions and monthly budgets
- Your financial goals and onboarding profile
- Your Supabase Auth identity

After deletion, residual copies may remain in encrypted database backups for
up to 30 days, after which they are permanently removed by our backend
provider's standard backup rotation.

Server access logs are retained by our backend provider according to their
standard retention policy.

## 8. Data security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your
data, including:

- Transport encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all network traffic
- Database-level Row Level Security (RLS) so that one user cannot read
  another user's records
- OAuth-based authentication (no passwords stored in the app)
- Session inactivity timeout that signs you out after 30 days of inactivity

No system is 100% secure. If we become aware of a personal data breach
affecting you, we will notify you and the appropriate authorities as required
by applicable law.
